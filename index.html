FAQ (frequentely asked questions)

Do you have gift certificates?

Gift certificates are available for purchase in the salon or over the salon or over the phone. If you purchase a gift certificate over the phone. we can either mail it to your lucky recipient or simply keep it in our files Elixir salon gift certificates are not redeemble for cash or refundamble.

Can I bring my pet along?

The State Board of Cosmetology and the Board of Consumer Affairs have specific Rules and Regulations stating that no animals are allowed in a salon at any time. Service dogs, not emotional support animals, with an official “Service Animal” vests are the ONLY exception to this rule.

What awards did you receive so far?

We recognise the successes of a wide range of talented individuals, businesses and team accomplishments within the county of Essex, and once again we are searching to identify those who are shooting for the stars and looking to put themselves on the map! Entering these awards not only raises awareness of the quality of hairdressing on offer, they create trust and loyalty with customers, reward and motivate individuals and teams, and act as a catalyst to attract new talent.
